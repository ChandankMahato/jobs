Welcome to the Job Selection Tool!

This tool helps you evaluate job opportunities based on your preferences. Select your job parameters, prioritize them, and calculate the rating to determine if the job is suitable for you.

If the calculated rating is:

  • 70 or above: You would definitely join this job as it meets most of your preferences.
  • 50 to 69: You would consider joining this job, but you would like to discuss some aspects further.
  • Below 50: You would not join this job as it doesn't meet your preferences.
